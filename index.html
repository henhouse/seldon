Seldon

An OpenLayers Web-GIS.

Seldon is a GIS viewer built upon the OpenLayers framework. Rendered with HTML5 & JavaScript it is compatible with modern web browsers and mobile devices.

Layers

Just click and drag with your mouse (or finger) to drag the graph up and down!

Multi-map views

Scroll in or scroll out using your mouse! Have hands? Just pinch to zoom!

Mobile supported

Just a few lines of code! Learn how to make your own graphs here!
